### P&L Calculation & Display
- **Auto-calculated P&L**: Entry price, exit price, and position size automatically calculate profit/loss
- **P&L Summary Cards**: Total P&L and Today's P&L displayed prominently on the dashboard
- **Cumulative P&L Chart**: 14-day area chart showing your profit trajectory
- **P&L by Emotion**: Bar chart revealing which emotional states lead to profits or losses

- **CSV Export**: Fully functional export with all P&L data

### Achievement System (15 Badges)
- **Bronze Tier**: First Blood, Triple Strike, First Victory, Discipline Initiate
- **Silver Tier**: Hot Streak (5 wins), Century Warrior (100 trades), Zen Master, Profit Hunter ($1K), Week Warrior
- **Gold Tier**: Unstoppable (10 wins), Discipline Master (50 disciplined), Profit Lord ($10K), Month Master (30-day streak)
- **Legendary Tier**: Void Sovereign (max guardian), Perfect Month, Samurai Legend (all achievements)
- **Toast Notifications**: Celebratory popups when you unlock achievements
- **Progress Tracking**: See your progress toward each achievement

### Functional Settings
- **Reality Check Alerts**: Customizable intervals (15/30/45/60 min) to check your emotional state
- **Meditation Cooldown**: Lock trading after 2-5 consecutive losses for 5-30 minutes
- **Sound Toggle**: Enable/disable notification sounds
- **Working CSV Export**: Actually exports your trade data now!
- **Dark Mode**: Fully functional theme toggle

### Animated Backgrounds
- **Samurai Battle**: Looping animated GIF background on the Dojo page
